What is Covid-19?

Since spring of 2020 a deadly virus has taken hold of europe. While its true origins remain a mystery, it is not unlikely to be connected to the rumored resurrection of Count Coronula. The virus, also known and classified as Covid-19 is dangerous due to its relative lack or varying degrees of symptoms. Normally infected people will at most experience mild headaches, fever, a loss of smell and taste or fatigue. In instances of heavier smyptoms like breathing difficulties it is strongly advised one seeks out a local cleric or the like to let oneself be examined. Covid-19's ability to spread is not to be underestimated, and protection measures are highly advised for all citizens, the same goes for staying at home entirely.
